**4. J&J gets EU approval for Imaavy in gMG**
- Source: pharmaphorum | 20251202T133323 | Bullish | Relevance: 100%
- Johnson & Johnson's Imaavy (nipocalimab) has received EU approval for treating generalized myasthenia gravis (gMG) in both adolescent and adult patients, making it the first FcRn blocker cleared for adolescents in Europe. This approval is based on positive results from the phase 3 Vivacity-MG3 and phase 2/3 Vibrance-MG studies, positioning Imaavy to compete with existing treatments from Argenx and UCB, while also having received approval in the US, Brazil, and Japan. J&J acquired nipocalimab through its 2020 acquisition of Momenta Pharmaceuticals, and the drug is being explored for multiple other autoimmune conditions with a projected annual sales potential of $5 billion.
